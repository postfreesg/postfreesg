The Florence Residences

Overview

The Florence Residences is a private condominium in the Hougang area. It sits along Hougang Avenue 2. The project is large and has many blocks. It offers a resort style living feel. Many families and couples look at this project.

Location and surroundings

The development is in District 19. This area is mostly residential. It is quiet compared to the city. There are schools, parks, and daily shops nearby.

Hougang Mall and Heartland Mall are not far away. You can find supermarkets, food courts, and clinics there. Punggol Park is also nearby. It is a popular place for jogging and family time.

Transport and access

The nearest MRT station is Hougang MRT on the North East Line. It is not within walking distance for most people. Many residents use buses or drive.

There are several bus services along Hougang Avenue and Upper Serangoon Road. Driving to the city is possible through the CTE or KPE. Travel time depends on traffic.

Project layout and facilities

The Florence Residences has many facilities. These include swimming pools, a gym, function rooms, and play areas. The layout is designed like a resort.

There are also garden areas and walking paths inside the project. This makes it suitable for families and older residents who prefer a calmer space.

Unit types and sizes

The project offers many unit types. There are smaller units for singles or couples. There are also bigger units for families.

Prices and value

Prices at The Florence Residences are usually lower than city fringe projects. This is because it is further from the MRT. Many buyers see it as a value buy for size and facilities.

Prices change over time. They depend on unit type, floor level, and facing. Buyers should always check recent transactions.

Who may find this suitable

This project suits families who want space and facilities. It also suits buyers who drive or do not need to walk to the MRT daily.

People who prefer a quiet area may like this location. Those who want city living may find it less convenient.
