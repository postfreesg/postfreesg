Why Use a Valuation Calculator?

A valuation calculator helps you understand whether the asking price or offer price is reasonable. It is especially helpful when comparing units across different projects, assessing a potential investment, or checking if your property is underpriced or overpriced.

  • Fast estimation within seconds
  • Useful for buyers, sellers and investors
  • Works for HDB, condo and landed properties
  • Helps avoid overpaying

What Is a Good PSF to Use?

The best PSF estimate comes from recent transactions in the same project. You can also check nearby condominiums or similar HDB flat types for reference. Using a realistic PSF ensures that your valuation estimate remains reliable.
