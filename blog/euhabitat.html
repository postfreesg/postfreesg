EUHabitat in Singapore

EUHabitat is a residential project in Singapore. It is an Executive Condominium also known as an EC. EC homes are built for Singapore citizens and eligible families. The price is usually lower than private condos at the start.

What type of homes are inside

EUHabitat has apartment units. The homes are designed for daily living. Most units suit families. Layouts are practical and easy to use.

The estate is gated. There are shared spaces for residents. These areas are for rest, play and simple exercise.

Location and nearby area

EUHabitat is located in the northern part of Singapore. The area is mostly residential. It is quieter compared to city zones.

Nearby you can find schools, shops and food places. Daily needs are easy to manage. Short trips cover most errands.

Getting around

Residents use buses and nearby MRT stations to travel. Main roads connect the area to other parts of Singapore. Driving to town takes some time but it is manageable.

For work trips planning helps. Many residents travel during off peak hours.

Facilities inside the project

EUHabitat includes basic condo facilities. These are meant for normal family use not luxury living.

You may find a pool, fitness areas and play zones. These are shared by all residents.

Who usually buys an EC like EUHabitat

Many buyers are first time home owners. Some upgrade from HDB flats. EC rules apply including a Minimum Occupation Period.

After a number of years the home can be sold on the open market. This is one reason ECs are popular.
