Euhabitat

Euhabitat is a large, 99-year leasehold condominium development located along Jalan Eunos in District 14, Singapore. The project was completed in 2016 and jointly developed by Far East Organization and Transurban Properties. It is known for its scale, modern design, and convenient city-fringe location.

Development Overview

The development comprises a total of 748 residential units spread across 53 low-rise blocks. Euhabitat is designed with a resort-style concept, featuring landscaped grounds and internal pathways that create a private and tranquil living environment despite its proximity to major roads and amenities.

Location and Accessibility

Euhabitat is situated within the Jalan Eunos landed housing enclave, offering residents a relatively quiet neighbourhood while remaining well-connected to the rest of Singapore. The development enjoys easy access to major expressways such as the Pan-Island Expressway (PIE) and Kallang–Paya Lebar Expressway (KPE), making travel to the city centre and other parts of the island convenient.

Public transport options are available nearby, with MRT stations and bus services within reasonable distance, supporting daily commuting needs.

Facilities and Living Environment

The condominium features a comprehensive range of facilities, including swimming pools, clubhouse areas, fitness spaces, and landscaped gardens. These facilities are designed to cater to families, working professionals, and long-term residents seeking a balanced urban lifestyle.

The low-rise configuration and spread-out layout distinguish Euhabitat from many high-density developments, offering a more spacious and community-oriented living experience.

Nearby Amenities

Residents of Euhabitat benefit from proximity to a variety of amenities such as neighbourhood shops, schools, eateries, and recreational facilities. Popular lifestyle hubs in the East, including Paya Lebar and Katong, are also easily accessible by car or public transport.

Summary

Euhabitat stands out as a sizeable, well-planned condominium development in Singapore’s District 14. With its resort-style layout, established developers, and strategic location near major transport routes, it continues to appeal to homeowners and tenants looking for city-fringe living with a quieter residential feel.
