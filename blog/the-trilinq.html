Overview of The Trilinq

The Trilinq is a contemporary, lifestyle-focused condominium located in the mature Clementi precinct. Known for its elevated sky decks, modern design, and functional layouts, it offers residents an attractive blend of comfort, connectivity, and convenience.

Key Highlights

  • Practical unit layouts suitable for individuals, couples, and families.
  • Convenient access to Clementi MRT, major expressways, and daily amenities.
  • Strong rental demand from nearby education and business clusters.

Location & Connectivity

The Trilinq sits along Jalan Lempeng, minutes from Clementi MRT and key dining, shopping, and community amenities. Its location provides a desirable balance of centrality and residential calm, appealing to residents who seek both convenience and privacy.

Connectivity is a major advantage, with quick routes leading to major business districts, shopping hubs, and popular schools.

Unit Layouts & Design
