High Park Residences

What High Park Residences is

High Park Residences is a private condominium in Fernvale, Singapore. It sits near Seletar Road. Many people choose this place because it is quieter than the city. It suits families and people who like a calm area.

Location and surroundings

The condo is in the Fernvale area. This is in the north east of Singapore. The place is mostly residential. There are parks and small shops nearby. It feels more relaxed compared to town areas.

Nearby places

There are schools, childcare centres, and neighbourhood shops close by. Seletar Mall and Compass One are not far. These malls cover daily needs like food, groceries, and simple shopping.

Transport and travel

The nearest MRT is Fernvale LRT. It links to Sengkang MRT. From there, you can reach other parts of Singapore. Buses also serve the area. Driving is common for residents here.

Project details

High Park Residences is a large project. It has many blocks and a high number of units. Because of this, prices were more affordable when first sold.

Unit types and layout

The condo offers different unit sizes. There are one bedroom units up to bigger family units. Layouts are practical. Most units are designed for daily living, not luxury.

Who these units suit

Smaller units suit singles and couples. Bigger units suit families with children. Some owners also rent out their units to tenants working nearby.

Facilities inside the condo

The project has many facilities. These include swimming pools, gyms, and function areas. Because the project is large, facilities are spread across the site.

Daily living experience

Living here feels peaceful. It is not crowded like central areas. Nights are usually quiet. Most residents are families and working adults.

Prices and value

Prices are usually lower than city condos. This makes High Park Residences attractive to first time buyers. It also suits buyers with tighter budgets.

Things to consider

The area is not near the city. Travel time can be longer. The project is large, so it may feel busy at peak hours. Buyers should think about daily travel needs.
