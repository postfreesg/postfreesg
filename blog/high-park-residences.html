Overview of High Park Residences

High Park Residences is a well-known mega development located in the Sengkang and Fernvale neighbourhood. Known for its wide range of facilities, practical layouts, and family-friendly environment, it attracts buyers and tenants who want affordability paired with convenience.

Key Highlights

  • Extensive facilities including multiple pools, gyms, adventure zones, and lifestyle amenities.
  • Wide variety of unit types, from compact units to spacious family homes.
  • Conveniently located next to Thanggam LRT, with easy access to Sengkang MRT and major expressways.
  • Strong rental demand due to proximity to amenities, schools, and nearby growth areas.

Location & Connectivity

High Park Residences enjoys a strategic position along Fernvale Road, offering seamless access to public transport via the LRT network. Residents also enjoy close proximity to supermarkets, shopping options, and dining outlets at Seletar Mall, Greenwich V, and Jalan Kayu.

Drivers can reach major routes quickly, providing convenient travel to business hubs and nearby towns.
