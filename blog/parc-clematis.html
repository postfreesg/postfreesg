Parc Clematis

Overview of Parc Clematis

Parc Clematis is a private condominium in Clementi. It is a big project with many blocks. The land used to be Park West condo. It was sold and rebuilt.

The condo is built by SingHaiyi Group. It has many unit types. There are small units and also family size units.

Where it is located

Parc Clematis is along Jalan Lempeng. This is inside Clementi. The area is mature. Many people already live here.

There are schools, shops, and food places nearby. Clementi Mall and West Coast Plaza are not far.

Transport around the area

Clementi MRT station is the nearest train station. You can walk, but it is a long walk for some people. Many residents use bus services.

Drivers can use AYE. It connects to the city and Jurong areas. Traffic can be busy during peak hours.

Schools near Parc Clematis

This condo is popular with families. One main reason is schools.

Nan Hua Primary School is within one kilometre. This is a big point for parents. There are also other schools around Clementi.

Facilities inside the condo

Parc Clematis has many facilities. It is like a small town inside.

There are swimming pools, gyms, and function rooms. There are also gardens and walking paths. Some pools are quiet. Some are more active.

Unit types and layout

The condo has many unit sizes. There are one bedroom units. There are also two, three, and four bedroom units.

Layouts are mostly practical. Some units face the pool. Some face outside roads. Higher floors have better views.

Unit Type Approx Size

Daily living experience

Living in Parc Clematis feels busy but complete. Many neighbours live in the same area. The condo has many residents.

Shops and food are easy to find. Clementi is a place where daily needs are nearby.

Who usually buys here

Many buyers are families. Some are staying near parents. Some want a school zone unit.

There are also investors. They rent out units to students and workers from nearby business parks.

Things to note before buying

The project is very large. Some people may not like crowded lifts or pools.

Units facing the main road may hear traffic noise. It is better to visit the site before deciding.
