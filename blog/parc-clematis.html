Parc Clematis

Parc Clematis is a large, recently completed (2023), 99-year leasehold private residential development located in the Clementi area of District 5, Singapore. Developed by SingHaiyi Group, the project comprises a total of 1,468 residential units spread across nine 24-storey towers, along with two rows of strata-landed houses within the development.

Location & Surroundings

Parc Clematis is situated along Jalan Lempeng in Clementi, a well-established residential town in Singapore’s west. The surrounding area is primarily made up of mature housing estates, schools, and neighbourhood amenities, providing residents with a balanced living environment that combines convenience and tranquillity.

The development benefits from proximity to Clementi Town Centre, where residents can access supermarkets, retail shops, food options, banks, and essential services. Major commercial and business nodes such as one-north, Buona Vista, and Jurong Lake District are also within reasonable commuting distance.

Connectivity & Accessibility

Parc Clematis enjoys strong transport connectivity. Clementi MRT station (East-West Line) is located nearby, offering direct rail access to key areas such as Buona Vista, Raffles Place, and Jurong East. Several bus services also serve the area, enhancing last-mile connectivity for daily commuting.

For drivers, the development is well-connected via major expressways including the Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE) and Pan Island Expressway (PIE), allowing convenient travel to the city centre, western business hubs, and other parts of Singapore.

Development Overview

Designed as a comprehensive residential enclave, Parc Clematis features a mix of high-rise apartment blocks and strata-landed homes. The wide range of unit types caters to different household profiles, from singles and couples to larger families.

The layout emphasises greenery, open spaces, and internal amenities, creating a self-contained living environment for residents. Building orientation and spacing are planned to optimise ventilation, privacy, and views within the estate.

Facilities & Lifestyle

Residents of Parc Clematis have access to an extensive suite of condominium facilities. These typically include multiple swimming pools, fitness areas, function rooms, children’s play spaces, and landscaped gardens. The facilities are designed to support both recreation and everyday lifestyle needs.

With schools, parks, dining options, and shopping amenities nearby, the development supports a convenient and family-friendly lifestyle. The location also appeals to working professionals who value accessibility to employment hubs in the western and central regions.

Investment & Living Considerations

As a large-scale, modern development in a mature estate, Parc Clematis appeals to both owner-occupiers and investors. Its proximity to educational institutions, transport nodes, and employment centres contributes to its rental and long-term value potential.

The variety of unit sizes and configurations allows buyers to select homes that align with different budgets and lifestyle preferences, making it a versatile option within the Clementi private residential market.
