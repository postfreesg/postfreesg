Overview of Kitchen Renovation Packages in Singapore

Kitchen renovation package prices in Singapore vary depending on design complexity, material selection, carpentry size, and whether the home is an HDB or condo. Renovation companies commonly offer bundled packages to help homeowners manage costs, improve budgeting, and achieve a cohesive design without unexpected expenses.

Packages typically include carpentry, countertops, tiling, plumbing, electrical work, and installation. Premium packages may offer branded fittings, quartz worktops, or customised cabinetry options.

HDB vs Condo Kitchen Renovation Costs

Renovation requirements differ depending on home type:

HDB Kitchens

  • More regulated due to HDB guidelines.
  • Wet works like hacking and tiling require permits.
  • Packages may include standard carpentry and basic rewiring.

Condo Kitchens

  • Costs are often higher due to premium appliances and materials.
  • Renovation timing and work hours may be restricted by management.
  • Dry kitchens and open-concept designs are common upgrades.

Tips to Save on Your Kitchen Renovation

  • Opt for modular cabinets to reduce customisation costs.
  • Choose durable laminate over expensive premium finishes.
  • Avoid unnecessary hacking from old layouts.
  • Buy appliances during major promotions.
  • Select practical designs that reduce long-term maintenance.
