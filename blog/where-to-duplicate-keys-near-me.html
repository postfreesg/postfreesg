Where to Duplicate Keys Near Me

Common Places to Duplicate Keys in Singapore

In Singapore, key duplication is easy to find. Many neighbourhood shops provide this service. Hardware stores often duplicate basic keys. Locksmith shops also do key cutting. Some shopping malls have small key kiosks.

Key Duplication in HDB Estates

Most HDB estates have key duplication services nearby. Look for hardware shops at the void deck or nearby blocks. These shops usually handle house keys and gate keys. Service is fast and simple.

Key Duplication in Shopping Malls

Shopping malls often have locksmith kiosks. They are usually near supermarkets or basements. Mall services may cost slightly more. The benefit is convenience and longer opening hours.

Types of Keys That Can Be Duplicated

Basic door keys are the easiest to duplicate. Gate keys are also common. Some car keys and special keys need programming. Not all shops can duplicate restricted keys. Always bring the original key for checking.

How Long Key Duplication Takes

Simple keys are done within minutes. You can often wait on the spot. More complex keys take longer. Some may need to be collected later.

Typical Cost to Duplicate Keys in Singapore

Key Type Typical Cost Range
Basic house key Around SGD 3 to 6
Gate or padlock key Around SGD 5 to 10
Special or restricted key Higher, depends on type

Things to Check Before Duplicating a Key

Check if the key has a do not duplicate mark. Confirm the price before cutting. Test the new key before leaving the shop. Keep at least one spare at home.
