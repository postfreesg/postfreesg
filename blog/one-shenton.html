One Shenton: A Practical Review of Location, Design and City Living

One Shenton is a landmark residential development located within Singapore’s Central Business District, along Shenton Way. Designed as a luxury high-rise condominium, it caters primarily to professionals and investors seeking city-centre living with direct access to business, dining, and transport hubs.

This article provides a practical overview of One Shenton, focusing on its location, connectivity, layouts, facilities, and what residents can realistically expect from day-to-day living in the CBD.

Location and Urban Context

One Shenton sits in the heart of the CBD, surrounded by Grade A office towers, hotels, and key commercial buildings. The location places residents within walking distance of major employment centres, Marina Bay, and a wide range of dining and lifestyle options.

Everyday conveniences such as supermarkets, cafés, fitness studios, and medical services are readily available nearby, although they often come at city-centre pricing.

The urban setting is ideal for residents who prioritise proximity to work and an active city lifestyle over suburban quiet.

Connectivity and Transport Access

Transport connectivity is one of One Shenton’s strongest advantages. Multiple MRT stations, including Downtown, Telok Ayer, and Raffles Place, are accessible on foot, offering connections across different MRT lines.

For drivers, the development has direct access to major arterial roads, although driving within the CBD can be affected by peak-hour congestion and parking costs.

The location significantly reduces commuting time for those working in or near the CBD.

Development Design and Positioning

One Shenton is positioned as a premium development, with a design that emphasises height, views, and exclusivity. Upper-floor units offer expansive views of Marina Bay, the city skyline, or the sea, depending on orientation.

The development has a relatively lower unit count compared to large suburban projects, which contributes to a more private residential environment.

Design elements focus on modern finishes and a luxury living experience rather than family-oriented communal spaces.

Facilities and Shared Amenities

Facilities at One Shenton include swimming pools, fitness areas, landscaped decks, and function spaces designed to complement an urban lifestyle.

While the range of facilities is comprehensive, they are geared more towards relaxation and personal wellness rather than large-scale family recreation.

Maintenance standards and property management are particularly important in preserving the value and appeal of high-end city residences.

Unit Types and Layout Considerations

Units at One Shenton are primarily designed for singles, couples, and smaller households. Layouts prioritise open-plan living, floor-to-ceiling windows, and efficient use of space.

Buyers should consider unit orientation carefully, as views, sun exposure, and noise levels can vary significantly depending on height and facing.

From an investment standpoint, the location supports strong rental demand from expatriates and professionals working in the CBD.

Daily Living Experience

Living at One Shenton offers unmatched convenience for those working in the city. Daily routines often revolve around walking to work, dining nearby, and making use of on-site or nearby fitness facilities.

However, residents should be comfortable with the pace and noise of city life, especially during weekday business hours.

Who One Shenton Is Best Suited For

One Shenton is best suited for professionals, expatriates, and investors seeking a premium residence in the heart of Singapore’s financial district.

It may be less suitable for families with young children or buyers who prefer quieter, low-density neighbourhoods.

Final Thoughts

One Shenton represents a high-end urban living option defined by location and convenience. Its value lies in immediate access to Singapore’s CBD, strong rental appeal, and premium positioning.

For buyers who understand the realities of city living and prioritise time efficiency and connectivity, One Shenton can be a compelling residential choice.
