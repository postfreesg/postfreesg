One Shenton

One Shenton is an iconic, 99‑year leasehold, mixed‑use luxury development located in the heart of Singapore’s Central Business District (CBD) at 1 Shenton Way. Known for its striking skyline presence and premium positioning, the development is a landmark address that blends upscale residential living with Grade‑A office spaces.

Overview

Completed in 2011 by City Developments Limited (CDL), One Shenton comprises two elegant towers rising 50 and 42 storeys respectively. The development was designed by world‑renowned architect Carlos Ott, whose work emphasises modern lines, functionality, and timeless architectural appeal.

Location & Surroundings

Situated along Shenton Way, One Shenton enjoys a prime CBD location surrounded by major financial institutions, multinational corporations, and government offices. The area is well known for its strong commercial activity, vibrant work environment, and proximity to Marina Bay.

Connectivity & Accessibility

One Shenton benefits from excellent transport connectivity. It is within walking distance to MRT stations such as Downtown, Shenton Way, and Tanjong Pagar, providing seamless access to multiple MRT lines. Major expressways including the AYE and MCE are also easily accessible, making commuting convenient for both residents and professionals.

Development Features

The development integrates residential units, office spaces, and retail components, creating a self‑contained mixed‑use environment. Residents enjoy premium facilities, while office occupants benefit from a prestigious business address in the CBD.

Investment Appeal

Due to its prime location, reputable developer, and limited supply of luxury mixed‑use developments in the CBD, One Shenton continues to attract strong interest from investors and owner‑occupiers. Its long‑term appeal is supported by Singapore’s status as a global financial hub and the ongoing transformation of the Marina Bay and Greater CBD area.

Conclusion

One Shenton stands out as a distinctive CBD development that combines architectural excellence, strategic location, and lifestyle convenience. Whether for residential living or investment purposes, it remains one of the most recognisable and sought‑after developments along Shenton Way.
