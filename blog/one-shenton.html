One Shenton

Overview of Keyword

Parc Clematis is a large-scale residential development characterised by its generous land area and comprehensive internal planning. The project is designed to provide a self-contained living environment, balancing private residential spaces with shared amenities and landscaped areas. Its scale allows for a more diverse range of facilities and unit configurations compared to smaller developments.

The development concept places strong emphasis on liveability, with attention given to spatial planning, greenery integration, and separation of pedestrian and vehicular movement. These design considerations contribute to a calmer residential atmosphere while supporting practical daily living needs.

Key Highlights

  • Expansive site layout with multiple residential blocks
  • Wide variety of recreational and communal facilities
  • Range of unit sizes catering to different household types

The highlights of Parc Clematis lie in its scale and internal diversity. A larger site allows for better distribution of facilities, reducing congestion and enhancing privacy for residents. This also enables more comprehensive landscaping, which softens the built environment and improves visual comfort.

Another key strength is flexibility. With multiple unit types and shared spaces, the development can accommodate changing household needs over time, making it suitable for both long-term occupation and adaptable living arrangements.

Location & Surroundings

The surrounding area of Parc Clematis is primarily residential, contributing to a relatively stable and established neighbourhood environment. Nearby low- to mid-density developments help maintain a balanced urban character without excessive commercial activity.

Essential amenities such as neighbourhood shops, educational institutions, and community facilities are generally within a short travelling distance. This reduces reliance on long commutes for daily necessities and supports a more efficient lifestyle.

Connectivity & Accessibility

Connectivity is supported through access to major arterial roads, allowing movement towards different parts of the city. This benefits residents who commute by private transport and require flexible travel routes.

Public transport options in the wider area provide additional commuting choices. While travel times may vary depending on peak conditions, the overall accessibility supports both work-related and leisure travel.

Facilities & Lifestyle

Facilities within Parc Clematis are designed to support a holistic lifestyle. Recreational spaces such as pools, fitness areas, and communal gardens encourage physical activity and social interaction.

The availability of multiple shared amenities also reduces the need to seek external facilities, enabling residents to enjoy leisure activities within the development itself.

Unit Layouts & Living Space

Unit layouts emphasise functional planning, with clear separation between living, dining, and resting areas. This improves usability and comfort across different household sizes.

Natural light and ventilation are key considerations, contributing to healthier indoor environments and reduced reliance on artificial lighting during the day.

Who Is Parc Clematis Suitable For?

Parc Clematis is suitable for households seeking a comprehensive residential environment with extensive facilities. Families, in particular, may benefit from the range of amenities and unit configurations.

It may also appeal to individuals who value internal convenience and prefer developments that support long-term residential stability.

General Living Insights

Living in a large-scale development often involves shared responsibility and community guidelines. This can foster a sense of collective ownership while requiring cooperation among residents.

Overall, Parc Clematis presents a balanced residential option that prioritises space, facilities, and internal connectivity, supporting a structured and comfortable urban lifestyle.
