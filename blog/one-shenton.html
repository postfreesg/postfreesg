One Shenton

One Shenton is a high-end condominium in the Marina Bay area. It is near the Central Business District. Many people know it for its tall towers and city views.

Location and surroundings

One Shenton is along Shenton Way. This area is part of Marina Bay. It is close to offices, banks and hotels. Many people who work in the city choose to live here.

You can walk to places like Marina Bay Sands and Raffles Place. The area is busy on weekdays. It is quieter at night and on weekends.

Transport and access

The condo is near several MRT stations. Downtown MRT Station and Raffles Place MRT Station are within walking distance. This makes travel easy.

If you drive major roads like Shenton Way and Marina Boulevard are nearby. Taxis and private hire cars are easy to get.

About the development

One Shenton has two tall towers. The project was developed by City Developments Limited. It is known for its modern design and full facilities.

Units and living space

The units range from smaller apartments to large luxury homes. Some units have sea views. Others face the city skyline.

Most units are designed for professionals and small families. The layout is modern and practical.

Facilities

Residents can use a swimming pool, gym and function rooms. There are also sky gardens and open spaces.

Security is provided. This gives residents peace of mind.

Daily living

Living at One Shenton is convenient. Many offices are nearby. Food options are easy to find in the CBD and Marina Bay area.

Supermarkets and daily services are a short drive away. Some people prefer to shop at nearby malls like Marina Bay Link Mall.

Who usually lives here

Many residents are working professionals. Some are expats who work in the city. A smaller number are investors who rent out their units.

This is not a quiet suburb. It suits people who like city life and short travel time to work.
