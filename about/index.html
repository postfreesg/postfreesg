Our Mission

PostFreeSG was created with one purpose — to give everyone in Singapore an easy, fast, and completely free platform to post and browse listings.
From property to jobs, services, and items for sale, we believe classifieds should always be accessible without fees or restrictions.

What We Offer

  • ✔ Free classifieds for property, jobs, services, and buy/sell items
  • ✔ No account required to browse listings
  • ✔ No posting fees
  • ✔ Simple, clean, mobile-friendly browsing
  • ✔ Direct contact between listers and interested buyers

Why We Built This

Most platforms charge listing fees or limit visibility unless users pay.
PostFreeSG offers a clean, fast, community-driven space where anyone can post and discover listings freely — without ads blocking the experience or complicated steps.
